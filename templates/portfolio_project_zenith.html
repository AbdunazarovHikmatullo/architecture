Project Zenith

Conceptual
Purity

Project Zenith represents the pinnacle of structural honesty and geometric precision. Nestled on a challenging hillside terrain, the structure utilizes a cantilevered steel frame that appears to float above the landscape, minimizing the environmental footprint while maximizing panoramic vistas.

READ FULL ARCHITECTURAL NARRATIVE expand_more

The interior spaces are defined by a singular axis that aligns with the summer solstice, creating a dynamic interplay of light and shadow throughout the year. Every joint, seam, and transition was calculated to sub-millimeter tolerances.

Materials were selected for their thermal mass and aging properties: raw board-formed concrete, blackened structural steel, and reclaimed cedar siding. These elements work in concert to create a residence that is both high-performance and deeply rooted in its context.
